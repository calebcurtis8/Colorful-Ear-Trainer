Instructions:
  • On iPhone deactivate the mute switch
  • Press PLAY, a I IV V I cadence will sound in the selected Key Center
  • 1 or more notes will play after the cadence
  • Play back the notes you hear after the cadence with the keyboard using your finger or mouse
  • If you don't answer within the time limit, your answer is marked as wrong and your streak ends. You must still answer correctly to move to the next question.
  • When it becomes easy, increase the difficulty by increasing the number of notes or by increasing the note set. Find a balance.
