Instructions:
  • On iPhone flip the Ring/Silent switch on the left side of your phone to the On (up) position or use headphones.
  • Press PLAY, a I IV V I cadence will sound in the selected Key Center.
  • 1 or more notes will play after the cadence.
  • Play back the notes you hear after the cadence with the keyboard.
  • If you don't answer within the time limit, your answer is marked as wrong and your streak ends.
  • You can play with the computer keyboard. Type the note name and use Right Shift Key for '#' or Left Shift Key for 'b'.
